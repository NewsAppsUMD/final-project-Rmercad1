Welcome to Argentina 329!

Argentina 329 is an electoral projections website based on the available polls for the upcoming 2023 Argentine presidential election and the provincial / autonomous city elections taking place throughout the year. The inspiration for this website is based on Philippe J. Fournier’s Canada338 and Nate Silver’s 538. Ryan Mercado is a journalism master’s student at the Philip Merrill College of Journalism at the University of Maryland. This website was created with help from Merrill faculty member, Derek Willis, and software Engineer Albert Najjar.
