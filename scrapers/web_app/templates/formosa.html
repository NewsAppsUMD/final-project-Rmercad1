Formosa provincial elections page

Provincial elections will take place in Formosa on June 25, 2023 to the elect the governor, and 15 deputies in the provincial legislature.Unfortunately, no polling is available for this election, however, we can show you which politicians in the provincial legislature whose terms are ending and are up for reelection. Whether the individual themselves is running for reelection or retiring is unfortunately not available. To find out more information, please visit the provincial government's website at: formosa.gob.ar/
